First case of migrants imprisoned in Nador: " sentenced to 10 years in prison when I've never touched sea water!!!"
Story of our comrade Hadi Elwali who met these migrants at the prison of selouane and who recorded their testimonies.

" Aziz Diabre prison of selouane in Nador, incarceration number 1989 of Burkina Faso. stopped by the civil Guardia of Melilla at the end of December 2018 in a successful attempt to skip the fence. Straight after his arrest, the Spanish civil Guardia took him back to the Moroccan gendarmerie who audition and signed him with tickets in Arabic.
After 2 months in prison, he was audition by the investigating judge who asked him one question: or and how did you get arrested? His answer was normal: stopped in Melilla after jumping the fence.
After a few days he was called by the Nador court. Two sessions without a translator at the end of which he knew nothing of the charges against him. It was only during the third session in the presence of a translator and in the absence of a lawyer that he knew he is being prosecuted as the commander of a failed zodiac at sea. During this same session he was sentenced to 10 years in firm prison. Back in prison crying and in a state of great sadness, poor Aziz only told us: " How can I be a zodiac commander, when my feet have never touched sea water... in Burkina Faso there is no sea. I made the road to Niger then Algeria before arriving at the forest of gourougou in Nador where I lived 3 years in which I made 5 attempts to skip the fence and now I have to do 10 years in jail for a crime I've never committed ".
After appeal the court has confirmed this 10-year verdict!!! And Aziz is still in jail hoping that justice will be done.
This example of how migrants are judged, shows how a hot refoulement that is illegal can be manipulated by the Moroccan and Spanish authorities to jail the victims to very long sentences.
Shit!!!.
