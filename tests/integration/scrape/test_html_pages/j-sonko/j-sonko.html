Mike Sonko.
Watu Wangu,

I today welcomed the festive season by donating Kshs. 1, 000, 000 to Traders of Nyayo Market in Ngara that was burnt on Sunday and property worth millions destroyed.

The amount is to assist them in the reconstruction of their stalls and ensure they resume their normal business life.

It was indeed sad for us all I just felt they should not welcome the festive season with such a lose.

Nawapenda Watu Wangu Wote and will always stand by you kila nikiweza. Enjoy Your Christmas Eve!!
