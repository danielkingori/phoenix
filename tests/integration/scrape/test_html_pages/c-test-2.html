NOCK- Olympics Kenya

Paul Wekesa (born 2 July 1967) Arguably the best Tennis player Kenya has had in history.
He won 3 doubles titles(ATP),
Achieved a career-high singles ranking of World No. 100
Reached two tour-level quarterfinals at Auckland in 1989 and Seoul in 1995
Reached doubles quarterfinal in the 1992 Australian open
Won bronze medal - 1987 All Africa Games in Nairobi
