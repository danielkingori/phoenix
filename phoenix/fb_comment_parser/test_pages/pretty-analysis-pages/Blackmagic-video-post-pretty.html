Josh Williams

In my experience, this happens when either one of the following happens. 1.) Your file has moved 2.)Your cache drive is full or 3.) A corrupt cache file. Relinking solves 1.). Clearing up space solves 2.). Right click on the clip in the timeline, select clip attributes, remove all attributes removes 3.) or go to Playback, Render Cache and Delete Rendered Cache. Unfortunately if it’s not any of those circumstances then I am not sure what other possibilities they could be. That’s just what I have ran into with that error.
